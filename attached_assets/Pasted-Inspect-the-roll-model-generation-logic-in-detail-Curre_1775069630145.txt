Current bug:
The app is generating the lower roll as a simple axisymmetric round/revolved shape.
Because of this:
1. 3D output is cylindrical
2. 2D flower pattern is not appearing
3. OD/width looks constant everywhere
4. The lower roll shape does not change around rotation
5. The model does not match the reference drawing/photos

What to fix:

- A revolve may be used only for the rough base blank.
- The final lower roll must be non-axisymmetric.
- The lower roll profile must vary with angle (theta), not remain constant for 360 degrees.
- The 2D flower pattern must not be generated from a circle; it must be generated from a spline/lobed profile.
- The 3D model must be built from multiple angular sections or station profiles and then lofted/blended.
