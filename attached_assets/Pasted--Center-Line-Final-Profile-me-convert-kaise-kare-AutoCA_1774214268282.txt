# 📐 2. CENTER LINE → PROFILE CONVERSION FORMULA

## 🔴 सबसे important formula:

```text
Outer offset = (1 - K) × Thickness
Inner offset = K × Thickness
```

---

## 📊 Example:

* Thickness = 1.2 mm
* K-factor = 0.33

### 👉 Inner side:

```text
0.33 × 1.2 = 0.4 mm
```

### 👉 Outer side:

```text
(1 - 0.33) × 1.2 = 0.8 mm
```

---

# ⚙️ 3. AutoCAD में STEP BY STEP

## 🔹 STEP 1: Center Line draw करो

👉 यही आपकी main geometry है

---

## 🔹 STEP 2: Inner profile बनाओ

👉 OFFSET करो:

```text
0.4 mm (inside)
```

---

## 🔹 STEP 3: Outer profile बनाओ

👉 OFFSET करो:

```text
0.8 mm (outside)
```

---

## 🔹 STEP 4: Radius apply करो

👉 Inside radius = R
👉 Outside radius = R + thickness

Example:

```text
Inside R = 1.5  
Outside R = 1.5 + 1.2 = 2.7
```

# ⚠️ 6. COMMON MISTAKES

❌ Same offset both sides
❌ Radius adjust नहीं करना
❌ K-factor ignore करना
❌ Outer line से strip निकालना

👉 Result:

* size गलत
* fit issue
* roll design fail

# 🧠 7. PRO LEVEL TIP
