MUJE LINE SE ROLLS BANNAE TAK KA STEP BY STEP SAMJAYE SARE RULLES KE SATH 

Bilkul. Main ab aapko coil line se lekar final rolls banne tak ka step-by-step full process samjha raha hoon — design rules, practical factory rules, mistakes, aur real engineering logic ke saath.

Yeh answer is tarah samjho:

Raw strip/coil → profile study → flower pattern → pass design → roll contour design → shaft/spacer design → machining → heat treatment → grinding/polishing → assembly → line setting → trial

1. Sabse pehle samjho: roll forming line me hota kya hai
Roll forming line me flat coil ko machine me chala kar, alag-alag stations ke through dheere-dheere bend kiya jata hai, aur last me final profile banti hai.

Typical line flow:

Coil stand → decoiler → straightener/leveler → entry guide → forming stands → calibration stands → cutoff → finished profile

Yahan sabse important baat:

Final product machine nahi banati, roll design banata hai.
Agar roll design galat hai to line kitni bhi achchi ho, profile sahi nahi niklegi.

2. Line se rolls banane tak ka poora sequence
Step 1: Final profile ko define karo
Sabse pehle yeh clear hona chahiye ki aap kya banana chahte ho.

Aapko yeh sab fix karna hota hai:

profile ka section shape

total width

leg height

lip hai ya nahi

corner radius

material type

thickness range

tolerance

production speed

required finish

Example:

Profile: U channel

Material: GI

Thickness: 1.2 mm

Base: 50 mm

Leg: 25 mm

Inside radius: 1.5 mm

Yahi design ka starting point hai.

3. Material samjho — yeh bahut important rule hai
Same profile ko alag material me same rolls se perfect banana mushkil hota hai.

Material ke hisab se behavior change hota hai:

GI sheet

CR sheet

HR sheet

Stainless steel

Aluminium

Aapko yeh samajhna hota hai:

yield strength

tensile strength

springback

ductility

coating damage risk

cracking tendency

Rule:
Jitna hard material, utna zyada springback aur utni careful forming.

Rule:
Patla material jaldi bend hota hai, mota material ko zyada passes chahiye.

4. Strip width nikalna
Roll design karne se pehle flat strip width nikalni padti hai.

Isme aata hai:

base width

side heights

lips

bend allowance

bend deduction

inside radius

thickness

Simple idea:
Developed width = sabhi straight lengths + bend allowances

Agar strip width galat nikal gayi to:

final size galat aayega

open section hoga

legs unequal hongi

cutoff size mismatch karega

Rule:
Kabhi bhi sirf outer dimensions add karke strip width final mat karo. Bend allowance include karo.

5. Flower pattern banao — yeh roll design ka heart hai
Flower pattern matlab:
flat strip se final profile tak har station me shape ka progression.

Example for 90° bend:

Station 1: 10°

Station 2: 25°

Station 3: 40°

Station 4: 55°

Station 5: 72°

Station 6: 86°

Station 7: 92° overbend

Station 8: calibration

Golden rule:
Profile ko ek hi baar final shape me mat lao. Gradually lao.

Flower pattern ke main rules:
bend distribution smooth ho

dono sides me balance ho

sudden bend jump na ho

edge stretch control ho

material ka flow natural ho

Rule:
Ek pass me bahut zyada angle dene se:

crack

edge wave

twist

roll marking

overload

6. Number of stands kaise decide karte hain
Yeh profile par depend karta hai.

Approx practical logic:

simple profile: kam stands

deep profile: zyada stands

lip profile: aur zyada stands

high thickness: zyada stands

hard material: zyada stands

Typical:

simple U profile: 6–10 forming stands

lipped channel: 10–16

shutter profile: 12–20+

complex sections: aur zyada

Rule:
Kam stands me machine chal sakti hai, lekin quality gir sakti hai.

Rule:
Deep section ko gradual stages me hi banao.

7. Forming sequence ka principle
Profile ke kaunse part ko pehle bend karna hai aur kaunse ko baad me, yeh bahut critical hota hai.

Example:

pehle side pickup

phir main bend

phir lip

phir calibration

Ya kuch profiles me:

pehle partial lips

phir web support

phir side walls

phir closure

Rule:
Sabse unstable part ko control do, phir uske baad next feature form karo.

Rule:
Agar lip pehle bana diya aur main wall baad me bend ki, to distortion ho sakta hai.

8. Roll pass design
Ab flower pattern ke basis par har stand ka pass design banta hai.

Pass design me decide hota hai:

is stand me profile kitna bend hoga

upper roll contour kya hoga

lower roll contour kya hoga

material kis path me chalega

kis jagah support milega

kis jagah relief dena hai

Har station ka roll pair alag shape ka hota hai.

Rule:
Pass design me material ko pinch mat karo, guide karo.

Rule:
Roll ka kaam material ko marna nahi, control karna hai.

9. Roll contour design
Ab actual roll geometry banti hai.

Roll contour design me yeh sab decide hota hai:

groove shape

male/female relation

corner radii

face width

flange support

relief area

overbend amount

entry and exit blend

Roll contour ke main principles:
9.1 Material thickness clearance
Rolls ke beech working gap generally thickness ke around hota hai, lekin actual profile aur finish ke hisab se set hota hai.

Rule:
Gap bahut kam hua to marking, jamming, coating damage.
Gap bahut zyada hua to forming loose, size unstable.

9.2 Radius sharp mat rakho
Sharp radius se:

crack

coating cut

stress concentration

Rule:
Minimum bend radius material aur thickness ke hisab se rakho.

9.3 Material ko trap mat karo
Contour aisa ho ki strip smoothly enter aur exit kare.

Rule:
Closed pocket ya trap geometry avoid karo.

9.4 Edge support
Side bends me edge ko sahi support milna chahiye warna edge wave aur flare aata hai.

10. Overbend aur springback compensation
Metal bend hone ke baad thoda wapas khulta hai. Isko springback bolte hain.

Isliye final stand me kai baar:

90° final चाहिए तो 92°–95° तक bend dete hain

phir release ke baad 90° ke paas settle hota hai

Rule:
Springback ko ignore karoge to profile open niklegi.

Rule:
Har material ka springback alag hota hai.

11. Symmetry rule
Agar profile symmetric hai to forming bhi symmetric honi chahiye.

Example:
U-channel me dono side equal progress se upar aani chahiye.

Agar ek side pehle zyada uthi aur dusri baad me:

twist

camber

side unequal

bow

Rule:
Symmetric profile ke liye symmetric strain distribution bahut zaroori hai.

12. Neutral line aur material flow
Bending me outer fiber stretch hota hai aur inner compress hota hai. Beech me ek neutral zone hota hai.

Roll design ka kaam hota hai:

material stretch ko control karna

compression wrinkling rokna

neutral line ko manageable rakhna

Rule:
Edge ko zyada khinchoge to crack.
Andar zyada compress karoge to wave/wrinkle.

13. Roll face width aur spacers
Roll sirf contour nahi hota; uske saath shaft, spacers, bushes, keys bhi hote hain.

Aapko design karna padta hai:

roll face width

spacer width

shaft seating

keyway

lock nut arrangement

distance between stands

Rule:
Contour sahi hone ke saath roll mounting bhi sahi honi chahiye.

Rule:
Spacer गलती se pass position change ho jaati hai.

14. Shaft design
Rolls shaft par mount hote hain. Shaft design weak hua to roll design bekaar ho jayega.

Check:

shaft diameter

bending load

torsion

bearing position

key size

deflection

Rule:
Shaft deflection se profile size change hota hai.

Rule:
Heavy profile ke liye shaft rigid hona chahiye.

15. Stand design and alignment rules
Machine stand alignment bhi roll performance me direct role play karta hai.

Aapko dekhna hota hai:

vertical center line

horizontal pass line

stand parallelism

shaft center alignment

bearing play

housing rigidity

Rule:
Agar line center galat hai to perfect rolls bhi defect denge.

Rule:
Entry guide aur first stand alignment sabse critical hota hai.

16. Entry guides ka role
Entry guide strip ko first pass me sahi center pe bhejta hai.

Agar guide galat hai:

strip one side khisakti hai

edge contact unequal hota hai

first bend off-center hota hai

poori profile twist ho sakti hai

Rule:
Guide ka kaam sirf feed nahi, centering bhi hai.

17. Calibration stands
Final forming ke baad calibration stands lagte hain.

Inka kaam:

final size lock karna

leg straightness improve karna

opening control karna

surface improve karna

Rule:
Forming stand aur calibration stand alag soch ke design karo.

Forming stand bend deta hai.
Calibration stand size pakadta hai.

18. Roll material ka selection
Industrial rolls aam tor par hardened tool steel se bante hain.

Common options:

EN31

D2

D3

alloy tool steels

special coated steels

Rule:
Soft roll jaldi ghis jayega.
Bahut brittle material crack kar sakta hai.

Roll material selection depends on:

production volume

material hardness

coating sensitivity

finish requirement

5. Strip width calculation kaise hoti hai
Yeh bahut important hai.

Flat strip ki width nikalni hoti hai jisse profile banegi.

Basic formula:
Developed Width = straight lengths + bend allowances

Simple U-channel ke liye:

Flat width = Web + 2 × Leg + Bend Allowance
Example:

Web = 50

Leg = 25 + 25

Bend allowance total ≈ 2 mm (depends on radius/thickness)

To approx flat width:

50 + 25 + 25 + 2 = 102 mm
Bend allowance ka concept
Jab sheet bend hoti hai to material neutral axis ke around deform hota hai.

Isliye outer dimension simple add karna enough nahi hota.

Bend allowance approx formula:
BA = (π/180) × Bend Angle × (R + K×T)
Jahan:

BA = bend allowance

R = inside radius

T = thickness

K = neutral axis factor (approx 0.3 to 0.5)

For 90° bend:

BA = 1.5708 × (R + K×T)
Example:

R = 1.5

T = 1.2

K = 0.33

Then:

BA ≈ 1.5708 × (1.5 + 0.33×1.2)
BA ≈ 1.5708 × (1.5 + 0.396)
BA ≈ 1.5708 × 1.896
BA ≈ 2.98 mm
Agar 2 bends hain to total bend allowance accordingly consider hogi.

Rule:
Strip width galat hui to final profile ka size kabhi perfect nahi aayega.

2. Strip width calculation
Flat strip width nikalna sabse pehla real calculation hota hai.

Formula idea:

Developed Width = straight lengths + bend allowances

For U-channel:

Web = 50

Leg = 25 + 25

2 bends at 90°

R = 1.5

T = 1.2

Approx bend allowance per 90° bend:

BA = 1.5708 × (R + K×T)

Agar K = 0.33:

BA ≈ 1.5708 × (1.5 + 0.33×1.2)
BA ≈ 1.5708 × 1.896
BA ≈ 2.98 mm

2 bends ke liye total approx ≈ 5.96 mm

To developed width approx:
50 + 25 + 25 + 5.96 = 105.96 mm

Practical trial aur profile definition ke hisab se isse slightly adjust kiya ja sakta hai.

Dhyan:
Agar outer dimension based drawing hai ya inside dimension based hai, width calculation ka method thoda change ho sakta hai. Isi liye production me trial correction normal hota hai.
